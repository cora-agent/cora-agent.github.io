CORA: Conformal Risk-Controlled Agents for Safeguarded Mobile GUI Automation

CORA brings calibrated risk control into mobile GUI agents, helping automation systems act more safely when interfaces are uncertain, high-stakes, or prone to cascading errors.

Overview

Why CORA

Mobile GUI automation is powerful, but a single wrong tap can trigger a sequence of costly or irreversible actions. CORA focuses on safeguarded execution by combining agentic control with conformal risk management, aiming for decisions that are not only capable but also calibrated.

The project targets a practical question: how can agents interact with real mobile interfaces while keeping the probability of harmful behavior under control? CORA is built around that safety-first objective.
